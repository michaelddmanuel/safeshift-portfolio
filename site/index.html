What's inside

An HSE program in a box

Every module is tenant-scoped, feature-flagged, and themed per brand — flip the 3-way brand toggle and the whole app reskins live.

Certification & Expiry Tracker

OSHA, HAZWOPER, H2S, LOTO and more — with a nightly scan, 30/60/90-day horizons, and pop-up cert details.

Toolbox Talks

Daily pre-shift briefings with digital sign-in — QR, manual, or virtual — tied to sites and facilitators.

Incidents & OSHA 300 Log

Near-miss to recordable injury: investigation workflow, root cause, CAPA, and an auto-built 29 CFR 1904 log.

Workforce Management

Add, edit, deactivate and reactivate workers — with per-person cert readiness and contractor gating.

Reports & Analytics

Audit-readiness KPIs, certification donut, expiry forecast, at-risk workers, incident breakdowns, CSV export.

Multi-Tenant Core

Request-scoped tenant isolation via AsyncLocalStorage + Sequelize hooks, JWT auth, feature flags, live theming.
